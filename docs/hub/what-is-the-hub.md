# What Is the Hub?

The Phlix Hub is an optional companion service that lets you reach your
self-hosted Phlix server from outside your home network without configuring
port forwarding or a VPN. You claim your server to a hub once, and the hub then
brokers remote access, library sharing, and multi-server management on your
behalf.

The hub is not required to run Phlix — your media server works fully on the
local network on its own. The hub only adds remote-access and sharing
convenience.

## What the Hub Provides

- **Remote access** to your server via the hub relay, so clients can connect
  even when your server is behind NAT.
- **Library sharing** with friends and family who have a hub account.
- **Multi-server management** from a single dashboard.
